Problem

Currently, only 9% of the world’s plastic is recycled (source). Plastics are littering cities, oceans and waterways. The consequences are health problems for both humans and animals, and now it's a part of our food chain. It takes more than 400 years to degrade, but it will never fully degrade.

What will the future look like if we don't change our habits?

Photo: "North Norwegian debate" (source).


Solution

Here in Norway we are doing a great effort recycling plastic bottles, why can’t we do the same with any other plastic? We have therefore developed the idea “compressIT”, in three sections (click on the sections for more info):

1. The Compressor

2. The Machine

3. The Application

We hope that our solution will motivate more people to recycle and help with the worldwide pollution problem.

Photo: "Slate.fr"(source).


1. The Compressor

“compressIT” is a garbage bin, with a lid that works as a compressor (visualize a French press). The plastic will be compressed as it fills up and therefore not be of annoyance. We believe that this will contribute to make a dull daily task to be fun, effective and easy to implement (even in busy and/or small households).

In order for the deposit arrangement to be “attractive” in households, garbage bins will be made in the usual size like the existing ones.


2. The Machine

A machine in the supermarket will register the amount of plastic through a built-in scale device. There will also be a sensor that scans the deposit to ensure that it only contains plastic.

The system will give you two options after depositing:

1. No phone? No problem! Print the QR code. You can either use it for a discount when buying items or withdraw the money in the store.

2. Not a fan of paper? No problem! The machine will show a QR-code on the screen, which you can scan with our app on your phone. Read more about this in "the Application" bellow.


3. The Application

There will be a comressIT- app (just like the Trumf and Coop membership) that will register the amount of plastic you have deposited so far.

In the app you will be able to follow a progress bar that shows you the amount of plastic you have saved the environment for, and your collected bonus. You can cash out the bonus at any time.


Research

Our research is based on a survey conducted amongst family and friends. The arrangement has a long-term perspective, which is to make people reflect and rethink the amount of plastic thrown away.

The survey was sent to 60 people with different background, age, sex and habits. Despite the diversity the same response returned: “Todays recycle arrangement is not very space saving». Therefore, many people choose to throw the plastic together with the residual waste.

In the «compresIT»-team, we all have a passion for solutions that will improve our environment and the recycling system, especially the pollution caused by plastic waste. The results from the survey showed that this is something that needs improvement.

"No plastic is fantastic. Do something drastic, cut the plastic. Handle with care, plastic is everywhere!"

Our mission is to reduce the plastic use/littering as much as we can.

Our vision is that “compressIT” one day will be a part of every Norwegian home.
